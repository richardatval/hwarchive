Leicester City NHS CCG Annual Public Meeting and Health Fair

Date: 08 Sep 2015 - 17:30 to 20:00
Location: St Martin’s Conference Centre, 7 Peacock Ln, Leicester LE1 5PX

Members of the public will be able to hear how local health services have performed during 2014/15 and learn about plans for the coming the year at NHS Leicester City Clinical Commissioning Group’s (CCG) Annual Public Meeting and health fair.

The event will start with a health fair from 5:30pm to 7:00pm, when visitors will be able to learn more about some of the health services that the CCG is responsible for, receive advice on improving and maintaining good health and talk to members of the CCG’s Governing Body, nine of which are GPs.

The formal Annual Public Meeting will take place from 7:00pm to 8:00pm. It will launch with a presentation covering progress on key healthcare targets, main achievements, spending over the last year and plans for the future to improve the health and life expectancy of people living in Leicester. The main presentation will be followed by an opportunity to ask questions of a panel of Governing Body members.
